ABOUT

We're a group of students partnering with award-winning faculty to build marketplace literacy in low-income, low-literate communities.

HELP

Microenterprise consulting leverages the talents and skills of our team to advance those who need it most. We work with frontier programs in maker and marketplace literacy to identify and accelerate microenterprise development.

TEACH

We've designed fourteen field-tested modules for those who live without the consumer and entrepreneurial literacy we take for granted. The result is an understanding of the context they live in and methods of marketplace navigation that can lead to potentially life-changing sources of income.

RESEARCH

Exposure is education. Our immersions in India, Argentina, and Tanzania provide life-changing experiences today to tomorrow's leaders. The relationships we've built over the years uniquely position us to unearth insights from the bottom-up with the world's leading researchers on resource-constrained economies.

From our signature immersion experiences to microenterprise consulting, we deliver a platform for social entrepreneurship and innovation. Our research and infrastructure are available to those interested in starting movements elsewhere. Be a part of our growth.
