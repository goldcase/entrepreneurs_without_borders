ABOUT

We're a group of students partnering with award-winning faculty to build marketplace literacy in low-income, low-literate communities.

RESEARCH

Create knowledge in a field off-limits to most. The relationships we've built over the years uniquely position us to unearth insights from the bottom-up with the world's leading researchers on resource-constrained economies.

TEACH

We've designed fourteen field-tested modules for those who live without the consumer and entrepreneurial literacy we take for granted. The result is an understanding of the context they live in and methods of marketplace navigation that can lead to potentially life-changing sources of income.

LEARN

Exposure is education. Our immersions in India, Argentina, and Tanzania provide life-changing experiences to tomorrow's leaders.
